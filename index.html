The Hadith

عَنْ عُثْمَانَ بْنِ عَفَّانَ رَضِيَ اللَّهُ عَنْهُ قَالَ: قَالَ رَسُولُ اللَّهِ ﷺ: «مَنْ بَنَى مَسْجِدًا لِلَّهِ، بَنَى اللَّهُ لَهُ بَيْتًا فِي الْجَنَّةِ»
◆

Narrated by ʿUthmān ibn ʿAffān (may Allāh be pleased with him): the Messenger of Allāh ﷺ said, “Whoever builds a masjid for Allāh, Allāh will build a house for him in Paradise.”

About the project

A new masjid, rising in Osogbo

Masjidul Ameen is a new masjid being built in Osogbo, Osun State, Nigeria, by the At-Tasfiyah wat-Tarbiyah Islamic Learning Centre — a community dedicated to tasfiyah, purifying Islamic practice in line with the Qurʾān and the authentic Sunnah upon the understanding of the salaf, and tarbiyah, raising a generation upon that practice.

The masjid is designed not only as a place of prayer, but as a hub for daily circles of knowledge, Qurʾān memorisation, and community gathering. It is a quiet, lasting investment — for Osogbo, and for the ākhirah.

The campaign is currently in its fundraising phase. Every contribution, large or small, brings the foundations a little closer to being laid.
